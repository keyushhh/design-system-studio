Pillar 01 · Visual Aesthetic

Editorial Precision & The Sharp Radius Rationale

Modern design systems frequently default to high border-radii (`8px` to `16px`), creating generic "card-heavy" interfaces. Design System Studio deliberately takes an editorial stance by enforcing var(--radius-sharp: 0px) as the foundational component default.

Pillar 02 · Perceptual Color Engine

OKLCH Perceptual Lightness vs. HSL Interpolation

Standard HSL color scales suffer from non-uniform human perceptual lightness (e.g. pure yellow feels brighter than pure blue at 50% lightness). Design System Studio generates scales in the OKLCH perceptual color space, ensuring smooth lightness curves across all 10 steps (`50` through `950`).

Pillar 03 · Token Cascade

The 3-Layer Token Cascade Architecture

Tokens are organized into three strict abstraction layers to decouple raw hex values from component implementations:

Layer 1: Primitives (Raw Specs)
--brand-500: #10b981;  ·  --neutral-900: #171717;

Layer 2: Semantic Aliases (Intent Rules)
--action-primary: var(--neutral-900);  ·  --surface-default: var(--neutral-0);

Layer 3: Component Implementation
<Button variant="primary"> → consumes var(--action-primary)
