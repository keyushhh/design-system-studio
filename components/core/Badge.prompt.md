Compact status / category label - badge, tag, or pill.

```jsx
<Badge tone="brand">Primary</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" dot>Active</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" variant="solid">Tentative</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" shape="square">Draft</Badge>
```

Tones: brand, neutral, success, warning, error, info. `variant` `subtle` (soft fill, default) or `solid`. `dot` adds a leading status dot. `shape` `pill` (default) or `square` for the editorial in-product look.
