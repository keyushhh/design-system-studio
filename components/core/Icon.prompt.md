Curated Lucide line-icon wrapper - render a product glyph by name in currentColor.

```jsx
<Icon name="share" size={15} />
<Icon name="chevron-down" size={12} strokeWidth={2.5} />
```

Available names: share, download, upload, eye, eye-off, plus, trash, edit, reset, chevron-down, check, link, x, copy. Stroke-based, round caps/joins, 24 viewBox. Use inside Button/IconButton or standalone.
