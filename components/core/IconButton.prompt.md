Square, icon-only control for row and toolbar actions - the hover-revealed hide/duplicate/delete buttons in the slide nav.

```jsx
<IconButton icon="eye" label="Hide slide" />
<IconButton icon="plus" label="Duplicate slide" />
<IconButton icon="trash" tone="danger" label="Delete slide" />
```

Tones: `neutral` (default), `brand`, `danger`. `size` sets the square hit area (default 24). `active` keeps the hover fill on.
