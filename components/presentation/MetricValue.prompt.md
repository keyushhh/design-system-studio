Oversized "data monument" statistic - huge display numeral, emerald unit, display heading.

```jsx
<MetricValue value="2.0" unit="M" heading="Total potential reach in one campaign week." />
<MetricValue value="95" unit="%" valueSize={280} headingSize={48} />
```

`valueSize` defaults to 420px (full-slide); scale down for inline use.
