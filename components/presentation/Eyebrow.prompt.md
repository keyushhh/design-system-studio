Editorial eyebrow label with a leading emerald rule - opens nearly every slide and section.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Executive Summary</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow center size={20}>Section Marker</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow size={10}>Part 01</Eyebrow>
```

Mono, uppercase, `--brand-600`, wide tracking. `center` for divider slides; `size` 10 for sub-labels.
